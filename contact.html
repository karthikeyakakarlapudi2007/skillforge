Frequently Asked Questions

Do I get lifetime access to courses?

Yes! Once enrolled, you have lifetime access to all course materials including future updates at no extra cost.

Our certificates are recognized by hundreds of partner companies. They're verifiable via unique credential IDs and can be added to LinkedIn.

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all courses, no questions asked. Just contact support and we'll process it promptly.

Absolutely. SkillForge is fully responsive and we have iOS and Android apps for offline and on-the-go learning.

How do I become an instructor?

Apply through our instructor portal. Our team reviews applications and provides full onboarding and production support.

Do you offer corporate / team plans?

Yes. We offer custom pricing for teams of 5+, including bulk enrollment and progress tracking for managers.
