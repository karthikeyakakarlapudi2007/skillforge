About SkillForge

We believe quality education should be accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Our Mission

Founded in 2020 by a team of engineers, educators, and designers, SkillForge set out to solve a fundamental problem: the world's best learning resources were scattered, expensive, or hard to apply in real jobs.

We built SkillForge as a single destination where ambitious learners can find industry-relevant courses, connect with world-class instructors, and achieve real career outcomes — all at fair prices.

"The best investment you can make is in yourself. We make that investment accessible to everyone." — SkillForge Founders

Today, over 50,000 learners in 120+ countries trust SkillForge to power their professional growth. We're just getting started.

Our Journey

2020

Founded & First 10 Courses

Launched with 10 beta courses and 500 early learners. Built from a shared mission to make expert learning affordable.

2021

10,000 Students Milestone

Crossed 10K active learners. Launched our first certification program in partnership with tech companies.

2022

Series A & Platform 2.0

Raised $8M. Rebuilt platform with live sessions, community forums, mentorship matching, and project-based assessments.

2023

Global Expansion

Launched in 40+ new markets. Onboarded 50+ international instructors. Crossed 25,000 learners worldwide.

2024

AI-Powered Learning Paths

Introduced personalized AI learning assistants, adaptive quizzes, and smart course recommendations.

2025

Today & Beyond

320+ courses, 120+ expert instructors, and a thriving global community. Building toward 1 million learners by 2027.
